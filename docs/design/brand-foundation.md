# MACS Brand Foundation

## Source of truth

The supplied MACS Digital Media logo is the governing brand asset. It must not be redrawn, stretched, recolored, or replaced without explicit approval.

Source image characteristics:

- Canvas: 500 × 378 pixels
- Transparent RGBA image
- Primary mark: geometric silver M/A structure with coral center accent
- Wordmark: blue `MACS`
- Descriptor: warm off-white `DIGITAL MEDIA`

## Core palette

The initial digital palette is derived directly from the supplied logo:

- MACS Blue: `#3A6EA5`
- Structural Silver: `#B4B4B4`
- Signal Coral: `#FE6E60`
- Warm White: `#F6F6E9`
- Basalt Black: `#050607`

Supporting interface colors may extend this palette with restrained Pacific Northwest neutrals, but the logo colors remain visually dominant.

## Visual direction

The main company website should communicate:

- Human trust
- Serious infrastructure
- Pacific Northwest context
- Client ownership
- Observable, controlled AI systems

Agent MAXX may use a darker cinematic control-room treatment, but the MACS corporate website must retain a human, documentary layer through founder imagery, the existing father-and-son video, Washington context, and clear explanatory content.

## Usage rules

- Maintain clear space around the complete mark.
- Prefer the transparent logo on basalt black, warm white, or restrained neutral fields.
- Do not add glow, bevel, drop-shadow, chrome, or 3D effects to the logo itself.
- Do not separate or reposition logo elements unless a separately approved icon lockup is created.
- Do not place the logo on visually noisy imagery without a solid or softly blurred contrast field.
- Generate favicon and social variants from approved crops rather than altering the geometry.

## Typography direction

- Display: Bricolage Grotesque or an approved geometric editorial equivalent
- Interface/body: Inter or Geist
- Uppercase is reserved for labels, operational states, and short navigation elements—not long explanatory copy.
